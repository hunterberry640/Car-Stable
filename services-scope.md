# Car Stable — Services Scope

## What We Are

Car Stable is a **concierge car buying platform**. We give everyday consumers access to the exact same dealer-only auctions — IAA, Copart, Manheim, ADESA — where franchised dealerships source their inventory, and we handle everything that comes after: financing options, title processing, transport, and optional rebuild coordination.

**Our pitch:**
> "We buy the car you want straight from the dealer auction — where dealers source their inventory — at auction prices. No markup. No inventory games. Just a transparent flat fee, and you get the car, not the runaround."

**What that unlocks for customers:**
- Any year, make, model, trim, color — if it exists in the auction ecosystem (millions of vehicles), we can find it
- Auction pricing instead of retail dealer pricing (typically 10-30% less on clean titles; 40-60% less on rebuilds)
- One flat, disclosed fee — no back-and-forth on sticker price, doc fees, add-on markups, or dealer prep
- Optional rebuild path — if the customer wants a branded-title car rebuilt to like-new condition for a fraction of clean-title cost, we coordinate that too
- Financing options, including lenders that handle branded/rebuilt titles that most credit unions won't touch
- Nationwide delivery via transport broker network

| We are NOT... | Because... |
|---------------|-----------|
| A traditional dealership | We don't hold inventory; we source per customer order |

| A consignment lot | Cars don't sit on our lot; they go auction → brief title transfer → customer |
| A financing company | We connect customers to lenders; we don't lend our own capital |

## How a Deal Works (End to End)

```
1. INTAKE
   Customer submits a "find my car" request via carstable.com
   — year, make, model, trim, max mileage, color preference, budget, condition tolerance (clean vs. branded)
   — pre-qualification or financing preference noted at this stage

2. SEARCH
   Car Stable searches live auction inventory (IAA, Copart, Manheim/ADESA via our dealer access)
   — usually 3-7 business days to locate 2-3 candidate vehicles
   — customer receives a shortlist: auction photos, condition report, auction estimate, our fee disclosure

3. CUSTOMER APPROVAL
   Customer approves a specific vehicle and confirms the purchase
   — they know the auction buy price + our flat fee + any add-on fees upfront before we bid
   — customer funds the auction buy (wire or ACH) prior to or immediately after the win, per our process

4. PURCHASE
   We bid and win the vehicle under our dealer account (Year 0: via FL partner's license; Year 1+: Car Stable's own TX GDN)
   — title is briefly in the dealer's name during processing (hours to a few days)
   — title is then reassigned/transferred directly to the customer

5. ADD-ONS (as applicable)
   — Rebuild coordination: if branded title, we route to a vetted rebuilder, manage the timeline, and deliver a rebuilt/inspected vehicle
   — Financing: if customer needs it, we connect to our lender network and coordinate the loan package
   — Transport: we arrange Central Dispatch or comparable carrier; customer picks up or receives nationwide delivery

6. DELIVERY
   Customer receives the vehicle + all title paperwork
   — customer retitles in their home state
   — Car Stable support available 30 days post-delivery for title/DMV questions
```

| Basic delivery coordination | Connect customer to transport options; coordinate pickup / drop logistics |
| 30-day post-sale title support | Help the customer navigate retitling in their home state; answer DMV questions |

## Vehicle Scope

Car Stable can source **any vehicle available in the dealer auction ecosystem**. There is no restriction on make, model, year, trim, or condition — if it runs through IAA, Copart, Manheim, or ADESA, we can find it.

| Vehicle type | Notes |
|-------------|-------|
| **Clean title — off-lease, fleet, trade-in** | The majority of auction volume; lowest complexity; no rebuild needed |
| **Salvage / branded title — repairable** | Collision, hail, water recovery, theft recovery, lemon law buyback; rebuild add-on available |
| **Branded title — as-is (no rebuild)** | Customer accepts branded title as-is; significantly lower price point; full disclosure required |
| **Non-repairable / Certificate of Destruction** | **We do not source these** — they cannot be retitled in the U.S. |

### Disclosure standards by vehicle type

| Type | What we disclose before purchase |
|------|----------------------------------|
| Clean title | Auction condition report, odometer, any structural flags |
| Branded title | Brand reason (collision/flood/etc.), auction damage estimate, our condition assessment, state retitling path |
| Rebuild path | Pre-rebuild damage documentation, rebuilder identity and timeline, post-rebuild inspection results, rebuilt title paperwork |

## Financing Options

Car Stable does not lend its own capital. We connect customers to lenders that fit their situation:

| Financing path | Who it's for |
|---------------|-------------|
| **Customer-provided pre-approval** | Customer comes in already approved by their credit union or bank; we just need the payoff instructions. Easiest path — we encourage this. |
| **Clean-title auction purchase lenders** | Lenders that finance dealer-sourced vehicles; most standard auto lenders will work here |
| **Branded/rebuilt title specialty lenders** | Most banks and captive lenders won't touch branded titles; we maintain relationships with lenders that specifically finance rebuilt vehicles |
| **Hard-money / private lenders** | For customers with challenging credit or unique situations; higher rates, but it gets the deal done |

We are **not** a finance broker, mortgage broker, or NMLS-licensed entity. We make introductions; the lender-customer relationship is direct. This distinction matters for state licensing compliance.

## Licensing Path

### Year 0 — FL Partner Provides Dealer Account Access

Car Stable's owner and TX business partner operate from DFW. **We do not yet have our own dealer license.** Deal execution works like this:

- **Car Stable LLC** identifies the vehicle, manages the customer relationship, coordinates all add-on services, and charges the customer the service fee
- **Florida partner dealer** (licensed FL IMD + SMVD) executes the auction purchase under their dealer account and briefly holds title during processing
- **Title flows:** Auction → FL partner (brief hold, days) → Customer's name
- **FL partner charges Car Stable:** $100/year auction access + $200/deal documentation fee + IAA pass-through fees
- **Customer pays Car Stable:** service fee separately; pays the FL partner (or directly to auction) for the vehicle cost

**Year 0 volume ceiling:** No hard statutory cap on this structure since Car Stable LLC is providing services, not buying/selling vehicles. However, TxDMV and FL DHSMV scrutiny increases with volume — stay below ~2-3 deals/month until the attorney review is complete and the structure is confirmed defensible.

**Top Year 0 priority:** Written agreement with the FL partner that precisely defines: auction-access scope, $100/yr + $200/deal + IAA pass-through fees, title-transfer mechanics, termination terms, and no retail-seller-of-record role for Car Stable.

### Year 1+ — Car Stable's Own Texas GDN

Once volume justifies it (~10+ deals/month), Car Stable obtains its own **Texas Independent Motor Vehicle GDN** (and Salvage Vehicle Dealer License if sourcing branded titles at meaningful volume). With our own license:

- We buy at auction directly under Car Stable's dealer account
- Title flows: Auction → Car Stable (brief hold) → Customer
- We can open our own IAA/Copart/Manheim dealer accounts at lower per-deal cost
- The FL partner arrangement is retired

See **[texas-dealer-licensing.md](texas-dealer-licensing.md)** for the full licensing checklist, cost summary, and timeline.

**Key GDN note:** Texas still requires a compliant physical location (commercial address, office, signage) even for a no-inventory dealer model. The "display area for 5+ vehicles" requirement is real but manageable — we do not need vehicles on a lot, but we do need a compliant commercial address. Short-term: sublet space from an existing dealer or use a compliant commercial suite. See texas-dealer-licensing.md for details.

## Geographic Strategy

### Now — Nationwide From Day One

Car Stable is **online-first from launch**. There is no geographic constraint:
- Customer can be anywhere in the U.S. (except states we can't ship branded titles to)
- Vehicles can be sourced from any auction yard in the country
- Delivery is coordinated via Central Dispatch nationwide

### Year 0 HQ — Dallas-Fort Worth, Texas

Operations are managed from DFW. Advantages:
- Dense IAA and Copart presence (Dallas, Fort Worth, Grand Prairie, Longview yards)
- Strong body-shop and rebuilder ecosystem for rebuild add-on coordination
- Proven branded-title consumer demand (AutoSavvy operates two DFW lots)
- Owner and TX business partner are local

### Year 1+ — Texas GDN, DFW Physical Office

A TxDMV-compliant commercial address in DFW (required for GDN) — not a retail lot with vehicle display, just a compliant office for dealer licensing purposes. We are still a no-inventory, order-based business.

### Longer-Term Expansion

Multi-state licensing as volume grows. Priority markets: Texas statewide, then Florida (existing partner relationship), Arizona, Oklahoma, Nevada. Each state requires its own dealer license.

## Service Quality Standards

Every deal, no exceptions:

1. **Full upfront disclosure** — before the customer authorizes us to bid, they see the exact auction buy price, all pass-through fees, and our flat service fee. No surprises at close.
2. **Condition report shared with customer** — we share the auction condition report and our own read on it before they commit to any vehicle.
3. **Branded-title disclosure in writing** — for any branded title, the customer signs a written disclosure acknowledging the brand reason, prior damage, and retitling requirements in their home state.
4. **Response SLA** — customer inquiries acknowledged within 2 business hours; shortlist delivered within 3-7 business days of intake.
5. **Title processing timeline** — title reassignment completed and shipped to customer within 10 business days of vehicle possession.
6. **Post-delivery support** — 30 days of title/DMV support at no extra charge.
7. **No-pressure buying** — we make money on the fee, not on steering the customer to a specific car. Our incentive is to find the right car, not the most expensive one.

## Open Questions / Policy Gaps

These need to be resolved before launch:

### Legal / Licensing

1. **⚠️ Texas dealer-compliance attorney consult** — does providing concierge auction-buying services for a fee require a Texas dealer or broker license, even in Year 0 where Car Stable LLC never takes title? Get written legal opinion before taking any customer money. Budget $1,000-$2,500.
2. **Written auction-access + title-execution agreement with FL partner** — defines the $100/yr + $200/deal + IAA pass-through fee structure, title-transfer mechanics, Car Stable's service-company role, termination terms. Attorney-reviewed.
3. **Customer payment flow** — does the customer pay the FL partner for the vehicle and Car Stable separately for the service fee? Or does all money clear through one party? (Has financing, escrow, and tax implications.)
4. **Financing referral licensing** — some states require a license to refer customers to lenders for a fee. Confirm Texas requirements.
5. **States we refuse to serve** — California rebuilt-title rules confirmed; full list TBD; attorney to advise.

### Customer Experience / Operations

6. **Base fee amount** — exact flat fee, tested against competitive market ($500-$1,500 range)
7. **Shortlist delivery format** — written report? Video walkaround? Portal login?
8. **Customer fund flow for auction buy** — wire/ACH in advance? Escrow? Timing relative to auction win?
9. **Rebuilder network** — anchor DFW shop(s) for the rebuild add-on; vetting criteria; quality standards; pricing transparency to customer
10. **Financing partners** — which specific lenders for branded/rebuilt titles + auction-sourced clean titles?
11. **Transport broker account** — Central Dispatch or comparable; rates; insurance
12. **States we can ship branded titles to** — built-out list with retitling requirements per state
13. **Return policy** — what happens if customer receives the vehicle and it's materially different from the condition report? Refund? Rebid?
14. **Insurance liability** — during the brief period title is held by the FL partner or (later) Car Stable, who insures the vehicle?

### Year 1

15. **TX GDN application trigger** — specific volume/revenue milestone
16. **Compliant commercial address** — short-term: sublet from existing dealer or commercial office; must pass TxDMV site inspection
17. **Own IAA/Copart dealer account setup** — application process, required documentation
