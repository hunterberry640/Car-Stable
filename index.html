Questions, Answered

Before You Trust Us With Your Next Car…

How is Car Stable different from a dealership?

A dealership buys at auction and resells to you for a marked-up retail price. We don’t. We charge one flat service fee to source the exact vehicle you want, and the auction price passes through to you at cost. You see every number on every deal.

How much is the flat fee?

Our base sourcing fee is a flat dollar amount disclosed before we ever bid. Comparable concierge services charge $500–$1,500. Add-ons (rebuild coordination, financing intro, transport) are each priced separately and transparently.

Can I see the auction condition report before I commit?

Yes. Every shortlist includes the full auction condition report, photos, and our honest read. You approve a specific vehicle in writing before we place a single bid.

What if the car shows up different than described?

We share the auction condition report upfront so there are no surprises on our end. For additional confidence, you can add a pre-purchase inspection — a third-party inspector at the auction yard before we bid.

Do you finance the vehicle?

We don’t lend our own capital. We’ll connect you to lenders in our network — including specialty lenders that finance rebuilt and auction-purchased vehicles that most banks won’t touch. If you already have a pre-approval, even easier.

Where can you deliver?

Nationwide. Clean-title vehicles anywhere in the U.S. Branded/rebuilt titles are restricted in a handful of states — we’ll flag any restriction during intake.

What if I change my mind after the shortlist?

No problem. You only authorize a bid after you’ve seen the full shortlist and approved a specific vehicle in writing. No approval, no bid, no commitment.
